Sai Anirudh Karre

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D (Part-Time) under Prof. Y. Raghu Reddy at Software Engineering Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad. I have completed my Master of Science by Research in 2016 from IIIT Hyderabad, published thesis in Software Quality Engineering. I have completed my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science from JNTU Hyderabad. I am currently working as a Program Manager at SkillSoft Inc, Hyderabad, India since 2011.
