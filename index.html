About

Software Engineer with a strong background in data products, responsive UIs, and backend APIs. Currently a Data Product Manager at Phenom Inc. Previously at Accurate Background, Cornerstone OnDemand, Skillsoft, and SumTotal Systems.

Guest Faculty at IIIT Hyderabad and research collaborator at the Software Engineering Research Center. Received PhD in July 2024 with a focus on Requirements Engineering for Virtual Reality Software Products, under the guidance of Prof. Y. Raghu Reddy.
